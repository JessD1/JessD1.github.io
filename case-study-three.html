Cirrus

A simple UI for a weather app that identifies polution levels.

I challenged myself to design a weather app interface that also provides updates on local air quality.

The app would provide a 7-day pollution and weather forecast to help the user plan their outdoor activity. I wanted to cater to sensitive groups such as people with asthma and help them achieve minimum exposure to pollutants. The app should be design to present the air quality data but also offer advice about what the data means in practical terms to the user.

I used Sketch app to design the user interfaces, inVision to create a clickable prototype and the Sketch Magic Mirror plugin to create presentation mock-ups.
