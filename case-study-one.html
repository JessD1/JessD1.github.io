Retire Online

A tool to help pensioners manage complex retirement options.

I worked with financial public corporation to design Retire Online, a digital product that would enable their pension scheme members to set up their pension payments using the organisations dedicated member website. I was a full-time employee in the digital services team.

The challenge was to create a product that made the complex process of retiring simple for web users aged between 55 and 75 who might be less confident transacting online.

The project looked to balance users needs with four key business goals, namely, enhancing the self-service offering on the website, supporting an organisation-wide digital transformation, reducing administration costs, and providing multi-channel service options to customers.
