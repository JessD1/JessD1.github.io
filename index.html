Hello

I’m Jess, a Londoner with a curiosity for digital design and human behaviour. I've spent the last 5 years working in tech as a web editor where I gained experience writing usable copy, designing useable interfaces and conducting user research.

Recently, I’ve taken all the useful skills I’ve gathered and started applying them to product design. My goal? To create delightful digital experiences for every type of human.

In 2018, I did a part-time course in front-end coding at General Assembly and built this portfolio to show you the UX projects I’ve been working on.

When I’m not UX-ing, I love painting, drawing and swimming in the ocean. I’m a huge advocate for positive social change and tech projects with a mission to make our world a better place.
